# Fellow Work KPI Context

Use these KPIs to identify which business outcomes are connected to a Fellow's work. A transcript may connect to one or more KPIs.

## Productivity

Connected when the Fellow increases useful output, reduces wasted time, completes more high-value work, or helps others move faster.

## Accountability

Connected when the Fellow follows through on commitments, reports progress honestly, owns missed outcomes, or creates clarity around who is responsible for what.

## Team Coordination

Connected when the Fellow aligns people, resolves dependencies, supports handoffs, coordinates schedules, or improves collaboration across roles.

## Process Improvement

Connected when the Fellow identifies inefficiencies, improves workflows, reduces repeated errors, simplifies steps, or makes work easier to execute consistently.

## Communication

Connected when the Fellow shares clear updates, asks useful questions, escalates blockers, documents decisions, or makes expectations easier to understand.

## Ownership

Connected when the Fellow takes initiative beyond assigned tasks, drives work without constant supervision, anticipates problems, or treats outcomes as their responsibility.

## Systems Building

Connected when the Fellow creates repeatable structures, templates, dashboards, documentation, trackers, SOPs, or tools that make future work easier.

## Execution Reliability

Connected when the Fellow delivers work on time, maintains quality under pressure, follows agreed processes, reduces rework, or consistently completes operational tasks.
